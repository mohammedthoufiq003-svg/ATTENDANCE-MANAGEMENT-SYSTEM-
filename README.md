1. PROJECT TITLE

Smart Attendance Management System

---

2. PROJECT OVERVIEW

The Smart Attendance Management System is a web-based solution designed to automate and simplify attendance management in educational institutions. The system allows faculty members to record attendance, students to monitor their attendance status, and administrators to manage academic and attendance-related operations through a centralized platform.

---

3. PROBLEM STATEMENT

Manual attendance tracking methods often lead to inaccuracies, data redundancy, and increased administrative workload. A digital attendance management solution is required to ensure accurate record keeping, efficient monitoring, and easy report generation.

---

4. PROJECT OBJECTIVES

- Automate the attendance recording process.
- Improve data accuracy and reliability.
- Reduce paperwork and manual effort.
- Provide real-time attendance tracking.
- Generate attendance reports efficiently.
- Enhance academic administration.
